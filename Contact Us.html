Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our system

Is this system free to use?

We offer a free basic version for individual teachers with core features. Institutional plans with advanced features and support are available at competitive pricing.

Can I try before purchasing?

Yes! We offer a 30-day free trial for all premium features with no credit card required. Experience the full system before making any commitment.

What training is provided?

We provide comprehensive video tutorials, live webinars, documentation, and dedicated onboarding sessions for institutions. Our support team is always ready to help.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use bank-level encryption, regular security audits, and comply with all educational data privacy regulations including FERPA and GDPR.

How do I get technical support?

Support is available via email, phone, and live chat during business hours. Emergency support is available 24/7 for critical issues.

Can I customize the system?

Yes, our system is highly customizable. We offer customization options for reports, workflows, and integrations to fit your institution's specific needs.

Don't see your question here? Ask us directly and we'll respond promptly.
